Personal Summary

Ph.D. candidate in Advanced Mechanical Engineering and Robotics at Ritsumeikan University, Japan. My work focuses on bio-inspired snake-like robots and compliant joint mechanisms, with experience in mechanical design, dynamic modeling, and system simulation using MATLAB and SolidWorks.

My research bridges theoretical modeling and practical engineering through prototype development, optimization, and data-driven analysis. Fluent in English and conversational Japanese (JLPT N3).
